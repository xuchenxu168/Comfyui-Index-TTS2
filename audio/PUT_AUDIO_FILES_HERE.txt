请将您的音频文件放在这个目录中
Please put your audio files in this directory

支持的格式 / Supported formats:
- WAV (推荐 / Recommended)
- MP3
- FLAC
- OGG
- M4A

音频要求 / Audio requirements:
- 时长: 3-10秒 / Duration: 3-10 seconds
- 质量: 清晰无噪音 / Quality: Clear and noise-free
- 内容: 单一说话人 / Content: Single speaker

示例文件名 / Example filenames:
- female_voice_01.wav
- male_voice_calm.wav
- speaker_happy.wav
- voice_sample.mp3

放置音频文件后，重启ComfyUI即可在节点中选择
After placing audio files, restart ComfyUI to select them in nodes
